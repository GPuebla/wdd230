Content

Home page

What is Ennari? We are a team dedicated to digital marketing through social media management. Our main objective is to boost the growth of projects and ventures through the creation of digital content that transmits a clear and friendly relationship with the user and meets the needs of each client, depending on the type of venture to which it is focused. How do we work? The process of developing marketing strategies is a particular process for each client and their needs but is mainly divided into 3 stages. 1- Analysis and objectives: We will begin by analyzing the type of project, the products and/or services offered and we will establish short and long-term objectives. 2- Strategic plan: A strategic plan will be drawn up according to the results of the analysis and a calendar will be drawn up establishing the distribution and type of content to be used during a given period (Monthly - Quarterly - Semi-annually). 3- Implementation: the creation of digital content, approval of these, and publication will be carried out..

Our services and plans

Our services and plans: - Branding analysis: Branding analysis involves examining the elements that make up a brand and evaluating their effectiveness in communicating the brand's value to consumers. This includes analyzing the brand's visual identity, messaging, reputation, and target audience. Through this process, businesses can identify areas where their branding could be improved and develop strategies to better connect with their customers. A thorough branding analysis can help businesses build a strong and memorable brand that resonates with their audience. - Creation and redesign of logos: Creating or redesigning a logo involves developing a visual identity that accurately represents a brand's values and mission. It requires a deep understanding of the brand's target audience and positioning, as well as careful consideration of color, typography, and imagery. A successful logo should be memorable, timeless, and versatile. - Creation and edition of digital content: Creating and editing digital media content involves developing engaging and high-quality material for various platforms, such as social media, websites, and blogs. It includes writing copy, designing graphics, creating videos, and optimizing content for search engines. Effective digital content should be relevant, informative, and visually appealing to attract and retain audiences. - Programming and publication of content on social media: A correct programming and publication of content on social media can have a significant impact on a business's online presence. It can increase brand awareness, engage with customers, and drive website traffic. Consistent and valuable content can also improve search engine rankings, establish authority, and build customer loyalty (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, WhatsApp). Our Plans: We offer 3 plans, which are already pre-designed, according to the needs and budget available to invest, but we can also work on a plan tailored to your needs. Plan1: Design of 2 weekly badges (Facebook and Instagram). Design of 4 weekly stories. Design of 4 covers for featured stories. Plan2: Design of 3 weekly badges (Facebook and Instagram). Activation of links in biography. Design of 5 weekly stories. Design of 5 covers for featured stories. Plan3: Design of 4 badges (Facebook and Instagram) weekly. Activation of links in biography. Design of 7 weekly stories. Design of 7 covers for featured stories. Biography writing. Adaptation of logo for profile image
